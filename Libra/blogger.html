Basic Information

Created:- August 2024

The Libra is a research library in kerala that is the national library of the india.It is one of the largest libraries in the world. It is estimated to contain between 170 and 200 million, items from many countries. As a legal deposit library, the indian Library receives copies of all books produced in different places in india,including a significant proportion of overseas titles distributed in the india and other countries.The Library is a non-departmental public body sponsored by the Department for Culture, Media and Sport.
